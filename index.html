Biography

Jian Yin currently works as a Senior Engineer at Bigo, focusing on Computer Vision. He received the master's degree in computer technology at Central South University (CSU) in 2022, under the supervision of Professor Xiaoheng Deng in Wireless Communications and Mobile Computing (WCMC) laboratory, School of Computer Science and Engineering (SCSE). He received the bachelor's degree in computer science and technology at Central South University of Forestry and Technology (CSUFT) as an "Outstanding Graduate" in 2019, under the supervision of Professor Zhufang Kuang in ACM laboratory, School of Computer and Information Engineering (SCIE).

His research interests include but not limited to:

  • Computer Vision
  • Computer Graphics
  • Deep Learning
  • Reinforcement Learning
